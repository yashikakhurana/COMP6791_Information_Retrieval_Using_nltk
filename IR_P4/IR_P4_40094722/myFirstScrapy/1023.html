Summer Programs and Extracurricular Activities

  • Summer programs and internships
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Student associations
  • Annual events

In what follows you will find a list of some summer programs, awards and internship opportunities related to Physics in Canada and elsewhere and available to Concordia students.

Various Fields
  • Volunteer or Paid positions in our Department and elsewhere at Concordia or in Montréal​
    How to apply: Browse webpages and contact professors

  • Concordia Undergraduate Student Research Awards Program (CUSRA)
    Location: Montreal, Canada
    How to apply: Contact professors by email
    Deadline: early February (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council (NSERC), Undergraduate Student Research Award (USRA)
    Location: Canada
    How to apply: Contact professors by email or meet them if they are in Montreal
    Deadline: mid-February (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • Supplements of the NSERC Undergraduate Student Research Awards (BPCA)
    Deadline: late April (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • Perimeter Institute, Summer program
    Location: Waterloo, Canada
    Deadline: early January (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • Mitacs Globalink research award
    Location: Various countries
    Deadline: Any time

  • California Institute of Technology (Caltech), Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowships (SURF)
    Location: California, United States
    Deadline: late February (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • Deutscher Akademischer Austauschdienst (DAAD), Research Internships in Science and Engineering (RISE)
    Location: Germany
    Deadline: mid-December (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Research internship program
    Location: Lausanne, Switzerland
    Deadline: December (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • Society of Physics Students (SPS), Summer internship program
    Location: United States
    Deadline: mid-January (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • University of Southern California, Summer Undergraduate Research Experience (SURE)
    Location: United States
    Deadline: early March (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • University of Tokyo, Research internship program
    Location: Tokyo, Japan
    Deadline: early January (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • Weizmann Institute of Science, Kupcinet-Getz International Summer School
    Location: Rehovot, Israel
    Deadline: late December (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

Astrophysics
  • Dunlap Institute for Astronomy and Astrophysics, Summer Undergraduate Research Program (SURP)
    Location: Toronto, Canada
    Deadline: late January (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • Institute for Research on Exoplanets (iREx), Summer internship
    Location: Mont-Mégantic (Canada), Hawaii (United States)
    Deadline: mid-November (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • European Space Agency (ESA), Student internships
    Location: Europe
    Deadline: mid-October (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

Biomedical physics and biomedical engineering
  • Institute for Biomaterials and Biomedical Engineering (IBBME), Undergraduate Summer Research Program (USRP)
    Location: Toronto, Canada
    Deadline: early March (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • University of Toronto, Department of Medical Biophysics, Summer Student Program
    Location: Toronto, Canada
    Deadline: late January (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

Particle physics
  • TRIUMF Undergraduate Student Program
    Location: Vancouver, Canada
    Deadlines: January, May and September (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

  • CERN Summer Student Programme
    Location: Geneva, Switzerland
    Deadline: mid-January (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

Thermoelectric
  • Highly Effective Applications of Thermoelectrics Research (HEATER), Summer Program
    Location: Canada
    Deadline: For the current deadline, please follow the link

Quantum Computing
  • Institute for Quantum Computing (IQC), Undergraduate Research Award
    Location: Waterloo, Canada
    Deadline: early January (for the current deadline, please follow the link)

Opportunities in the United States (mostly for United States citizens)
  • American Physical Society list of internships and fellowships

Concordia students have at their disposal many opportunities for them to develop professional skills and have hands-on experiences that will help them become competitive members of the workforce. In what follows you will find a list of communities within Concordia that have extracurricular activities.

Student led
  • Space Concordia
  • ConcordAI – Artificial Intelligence Concordia
  • Data Innovation Playground of Concordia
Concordia led
  • District 3 Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship
  • PERFORM Centre
  • Technology Sandbox

In what follows you will find a list of organizations that actively represent and advocate for certain students groups within Concordia. They also promote social events and organize academic activities.

Undergraduate
  • CUBCAPS – Concordia Undergraduate Biochemistry Chemistry and Physics Society
  • CSU – Concordia Student Union
  • ASFA – Arts and Science Federation of Associations
Graduate
  • GSA – Graduate Student Association
  • GPA – Graduate Physics Association

Some annual events related to physics are listed below. Students that will present posters or papers at academic conferences can receive financial support by applying to the Student Conference Travel Support from the Faculty of Arts and Science.

  • CCUWiP – Canadian Conference for Undergraduate Women in Physics
    When:  mid-January (for the current/next date, please follow the link)

  • CUPC – Canadian Undergraduate Physics Conference
    When: early November (for the current/next date, please follow the link)

  • SAPHARI – Annual Colloquium in Physics for a Future in Research and Industry
    When: early March (for the current/next date, please follow the link)

  • Physics Games
    When: early January (for the current/next date, please follow the link)

  • PLANCKS
    When: early May (for the current/next date, please follow the link)
