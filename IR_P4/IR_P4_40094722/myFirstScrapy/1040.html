Information For PT Faculty

Please read this important information regarding the application procedures for part-time faculty courses:

Postings for teaching positions available for part-time faculty can be viewed on the Faculty Resource Information System (FRIS).  All available PT faculty teaching positions across the university are on FRIS. Hard copies of postings can be viewed in each department as well.  Login into FRIS with your netname and password.

Users who do not have a netname may request a temporary account from the log-on page by selecting “ Click here if you don't know/have Netname". Follow the instructions.

If this is your first time applying to teach a specific course, please provide documentation about your qualifications and suitability to teach the course.

Interested applicants can apply directly online on FRIS.

NOTE: "Only applicants who are defined as Canadian per the definition below at the time of application for a part-time contract shall be given consideration by the PTHC. 'Canadian' means a person who is a citizen of Canada, or who on the date of application for a part-time contract at Concordia University is a permanent resident, or holds a ministerial permit as a consequence of being a refugee or being prohibited from applying for a permanent resident status." [Article 10.01a of the CUPFA Collective Agreement]
