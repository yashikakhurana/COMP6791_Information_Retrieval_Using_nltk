PERFORM Centre

Advanced Brain Imaging for the Study of Health Behaviors:
Sleep, Nutrition and Physical Activity

An OHBM Satellite Symposium

 

CANCELLED

 

After careful consideration, the PERFORM Centre has decided to cancel the OHBM Satellite Symposium we were planning to hold on June 25, 2020. This difficult decision was made in light of the extraordinary circumstances we find ourselves due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Though this event cannot be held as expected, we will try to have some talks from our invited speakers at a later date, perhaps in the form of a webinar if current travel restrictions remain in effect.

Details will be announced as we plan our program for next year.

We wish to thank you for your patience and understanding as we navigate these uncharted waters together.

 

Kind regards,

Habib Benali, PhD
Scientific Director, PERFORM Centre
Canada Research Chair - Tier I, in Biomedical Imaging and Healthy Aging (NSERC)
Professor, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering
Concordia University

Concordia University and the PERFORM Centre are pleased to be hosting this satellite symposium, in conjunction with the Organization of Human Brain Mapping.

 

"Advanced brain imaging for the study of health behaviors:  sleep, nutrition and physical activity" is coming to Concordia on June 25th, 2020.

Save the Date
