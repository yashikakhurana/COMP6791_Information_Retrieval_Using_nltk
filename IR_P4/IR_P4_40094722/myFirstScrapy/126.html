Concordia International Scholars

Every year Concordia University welcomes exceptional international students from all over the world to study, research and innovate at our thriving institution.

The Concordia International Scholars award is a newly established scholarship designed to support international undergraduate students who have shown resilience and the ability to overcome personal hardship while preserving and achieving academic excellence in their studies.

Each year, this prestigious award will be granted to up to 2 incoming students from any faculty.

IMPORTANT: You must apply for admissions prior to applying for this scholarship.

Scholarship Details

This scholarship will cover tuition and fees and is normally renewable up to four years assuming the students meet all renewal requirements.

Eligibility

  • First time attending university.
  • Studying full-time in an undergraduate degree program.*
  • International student, paying full international student rate.
  • Starting studies at Concordia University in Fall 2021.

* As defined by Concordia University‚Äôs Undergraduate Calendar  

How to Apply

In order to apply, applicants must complete the application package and upload it to your MyConcordia student portal (https://www.concordia.ca/students/financial-support/scholarships-awards/upload-process-for-concordia-application-based-awards.html). The package must be a single PDF file comprised of the following:

  • The Student Application Form
  • A current curriculum vitae that outlines all their work, volunteer experience, accolades, cultural, and other accomplishments.
  • A Personal Statement ‚Äì express how you have shown resilience and overcome personal hardship while maintaining an outstanding academic performance. Maximum 1000 words.
  • File name: LastName_FirstName_StudentID# (Example: Doe_John_12345678)

Due Date

All application packages must be upload it to your MyConcordia student portal by 23:59 EST, Monday, February 1st, 2021.

 

 

Selection Process

 

Upon receipt of complete application, applicants will receive a confirmation email from the Financial Aid and Awards Office. Incomplete applications will be disqualified without notice. All complete applications will be reviewed by Concordia‚Äôs Undergraduate Scholarships and Awards Committee. Decisions will be made and communicated to applicants via email.
