CONCORDIA UNIVERSITY PRESS

A non-profit publisher of peer-reviewed books that cross disciplinary boundaries and propel scholarly inquiry into new areas.

Concordia University Press books are engaged, accessible, and make a point. The Press publishes in English and in French. Launched in 2016, the Press is interested in proposals for books and pamphlets in the arts, humanities, and social sciences that engage with the themes of Life, Knowledge, and Creation.
