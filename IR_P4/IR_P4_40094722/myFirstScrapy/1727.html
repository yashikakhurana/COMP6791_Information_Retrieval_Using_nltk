University Advancement

Under the leadership of the vice-president, University Advancement (UA) builds relationships with the community and fundraises for Concordia.

UA organizes events for alumni, students, donors and friends and builds relationships with the community.

UA publishes the monthly Accent newsletter and Concordia University Magazine in winter, spring and fall for more than 220,000 alumni and friends.

UA collaborates with the Concordia University Alumni Association to offer alumni and students a rich and varied selection of benefits and services as well as social, educational and cultural events, including Homecoming, class reunions and networking opportunities.

Information collected via our website or email is subject to our privacy policy.
