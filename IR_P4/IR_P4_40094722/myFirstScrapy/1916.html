Student Success Centre

FROM FIRST YEAR TO GRADUATION

The Student Success Centre (SSC) gives you the tools you need to succeed – from time management and study strategies to building your network and taking your studies one step further, we’ve got you covered. Come see us — we can help!

Our values

Student empowerment through peer-to-peer and professional support

Fostering relationships and collaboration across the university and beyond

Respecting and understanding Concordia's diversity
