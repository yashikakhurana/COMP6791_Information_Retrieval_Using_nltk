Birks Student Service Centre requests

In the face of the COVID-19 pandemic, requests that were previously made in-person at the Birks Student Service Centre can now be made online. In very specific cases, an in-person appointment can be reserved. Below you will find key information and steps to follow to make these requests.

You can call 514-848-2424 x 2668 Monday to Friday between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m. EST with any questions. Otherwise you can email students@concordia.ca.

Important notes:

  • In-person appointments can only be scheduled via the MyConcordia portal.
    MyConcordia.ca > Student Services > Birks Appointment Booking
    You must have a valid netname. If you encounter any difficulties with your netname and/or password, please contact our service desk.
  • You must login to Concordia Office 365 to submit an online request. Instructions can be found on Office 365 Education - students.

Online requests

Tuition payment

Fall 2020 tuition is due as of November 1, 2020, and must be paid by November 30, 2020.
Winter 2021 tuition is due as of March 1, 2021, and must be paid by March 31, 2021.

Tuition can be paid remotely in numerous manners.

If paying by cheque, please send by mail to:

Birks Student Service Centre
Concordia University
1400 de Maisonneuve Blvd W, LB-185
Montreal, Quebec
H3G 1M8

Though not a preferred option, cheque payments can be left in the Birks mail slot without an appointment. Entry is via 1453 Mackay street, and Security must be advised of the purpose of the visit. The cheque can be left in the mail slot right by LB 185 in the lobby of the atrium. There is a sign just beneath the mail slot, indicating its location.

Cheques must be addressed to “Concordia University” and the student ID number should appear on the “memo” line.

A receipt can be issued following the processing of your cheque payment, if desired.

Proof of enrolment (attestation letter)

  • Attestation letters are only being issued electronically right now.
    Please see How to request an attestation letter.
  • Please allow 5 business days for delivery of your letter.
  • A $20 (CAD) fee will be charged to your account for any letter request.

OPUS card

  • The STM uses the Concordia ID card photo in order to produce the OPUS card. Please ensure that your ID card is created before submitting an OPUS request.
  • After 24 hours, the OPUS card can be ordered online and the STM will send it by mail.
  • To be eligible, a student must have a provincial (Quebec) home address saved in their MyConcordia Student Centre and be registered full-time in the current term (at least 12 credits). Note that wait-listed courses do not count towards enrolment.
  • To request an OPUS card:
    • From MyConcordia click on Student Services, Online OPUS Card Requests
    • Follow the instructions and make a payment of $15 to the STM via the OPUS platform

Degree completion letter or curriculum evaluation

  • Degree completion and curriculum evaluation letters are only being issued electronically right now. Requests can be submitted online: Degree Letter Request*
  • Please allow at least 10 business days for delivery of your letter.
  • A $20 (CAD) fee will be charged to your account for any letter request.

*Please note that you must login to Concordia Office 365 to submit your request. Instructions can be found on Office 365 Education - students.

Forms and other university seal-bearing documents

  • Scholarship and enrolment forms can be completed with the university seal.
  • The preferred approach is by email (students@concordia.ca). You may email your form to the Birks team and it will be returned to you in PDF format within 5 business days.
  • Form completion carries a $20 fee.

Unofficial transcripts

  • Unofficial transcripts are only being issued electronically. They cannot be issued with the university seal.
  • Requests can be submitted online: Unofficial transcript request form*
  • Please allow 5 business days for delivery of your transcript.
  • A $5.30 (CAD) fee will be charged to your account.

*Please note that you must login to Concordia Office 365 to submit your request. Instructions can be found on Office 365 Education - students.

Official transcripts

  • Official transcripts are available by mail and by courier (additional fees applicable if courier is chosen). In-person pick-up is not possible due to restricted access to campus.
  • Transcripts destined to other universities in Quebec will be sent electronically.
  • Official transcripts can be sent electronically to institutions outside of Quebec if the institution’s e-mail address is included in the request. Please verify with the institution that it will be accepted in this format. (Please note: One email address per request).
  • Instructions for submitting such a request, please see Requesting official transcripts.
  • Official transcripts carry a cost of $12.77 (CAD).
  • For urgent matters, please e-mail transcripts@concordia.ca.

Account summary

Available free of charge via your MyConcordia Student Centre. Choose “Account Inquiry” to obtain this document.

Letter of Financial Standing

  • Can be requested via MyConcordia Student Centre.
  • A $16 (CAD) fee will be charged to your account.
  • Please email studentaccounts@concordia.ca with any questions.

Exam requests (MED/DEF/supplemental examinations)

  • Request instructions can be found below:
    • Exam deferral (DEF)
    • MED notation
    • Supplemental examinations

Late-completion (INC)

  • Proof of your professor’s approval is required if the grade has not yet been assigned or if it is not accompanied by the INC notation.
  • This request carries a $30 (CAD) charge that will be applied to your account.
  • Requests can be submitted online: Application for Late-Completion Grade*

*Please note that you must login to Concordia Office 365 to submit your request. Instructions can be found on Office 365 Education - students.

Academic re-evaluation

  • If you are considering submitting a request for academic re-evaluation, please call 514-848-2424 x 2668 first in order to discuss the matter with one of the Birks team members.
  • Should you choose to pursue the request, you may submit it online: Academic Re-evaluation Request*
  • This request carries a $42.50 (CAD) charge that will be applied to your account.

*Please note that you must login to Concordia Office 365 to submit your request. Instructions can be found on Office 365 Education - students.

In-person appointments

Sworn statement (Commissioner of oaths) for Quebec Residency

  • An appointment can be made in order to discuss your Quebec Residency situation or to present a sworn statement to a Commissioner of oaths.
  • Appointments can be booked via your MyConcordia portal under Student Services (Birks Appointment Booking)
  • If you would like a sworn statement to be witnessed for AFE purposes, please communicate with the Financial Aid and Awards Office to ensure you are preparing the proper documents before scheduling a Birks appointment.

Diplomas (replacement or certified copies)

Replacement diplomas
Please see our diplomas website for more information and for the necessary forms. You can submit your request and provide a sworn statement at Birks by appointment. There is a $100 fee for a replacement diploma.

Certified copy request for Spring 2020 graduates
If you are requesting a certified copy, please ensure that you submit your form before providing your Diploma Mailing address in your MyConcordia portal.

Certified copy of diploma (degree conferred prior to May 2020)
Your original diploma is required for this service. Can be submitted by mail or in-person at the Birks Student Service Center. For in-person appointments, the Birks team will issue a copy onsite at a cost of $10 per copy.

Student ID cards

  • Student ID cards are being issued by appointment only. Drop-ins will not be accepted.
    MyConcordia.ca > Student Services > ID Card Appointment Booking
  • A new ID card carries a cost of $12.25. A replacement for a lost ID card carries a cost of $16 (fees charged directly to your account).
  • Please ensure that you are currently registered for at least one course for 24 hours before your appointment.
  • Appointments can be booked via your MyConcordia portal under Student Services (ID Card Appointment Booking)
