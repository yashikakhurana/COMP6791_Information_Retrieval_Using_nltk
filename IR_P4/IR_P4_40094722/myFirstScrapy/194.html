Webinars

Archive of PERFORM's online webinars and colloquia

The PERFORM Colloquium series presents ongoing research talks on a broad range of topics related to preventative health research. Although these events are intended for an Academic & Researcher audience, everyone is welcome.
We have also been hosting webinars for the general public on topics such as cooking healthy meals at home, exercise strategies, sleep, and mental health.

Subscribe to our YouTube channel for more webinars as they are posted. There is a dedicated playlist there for just the webinars.
