The Montreal Math Circle

Session 1 starts on October 4, 2020.

Organizer / Organisatrice:  Ildiko Pelczer (montrealmathclub@gmail.com)


We will be offering a fall session of 8 classes for the 2020-2021 Montreal Math Circle starting October 4.  Please refer to the schedule listed below.  If you would like to register your child for this session, please complete the form https://forms.gle/qRr6tuL9BFTmz2HG8.  A separate form must be completed for each child you wish to register. In order to properly plan this session, we kindly ask you to register your child no later than September 28h. If by this date we have less than 6 registrations for a specific level, we must cancel that level and will inform you via email at the address you provided at registration.

Cost: There will be a one-time registration fee of $100 per child for the session (taxes included). Payment procedure will be communicated shortly. The Montreal Math Circle reserves its right to cancel a certain level if a minimum of 6 participants is not attained.

Dates:         On Sundays / Les dimanches (October 4 - November 29)
Times:         See times for each grade below.
Place:          Via Zoom. Link TBA

Registration Payment

 

Montreal Math Circle is on Facebook
https://www.facebook.com/MontrealMathCircle/

News:  Congratulations to Montreal Math Circle students for their results at the 2019 Canadian Open Mathematics Challenge (COMC):
- Allison Tsypin:  Gold award Quebec (grade 11)
- Alexei Kieran:  Bronze award Quebec (grade 11)
- Anna Shi:  Honor Roll Quebec (grade 11)
- Leo Vanciu:  Performance with Honors (grade 11)
- Fangrui Xu:  Bronze award Quebec (grade 8 and under)

To view COMC's national winners:   https://cms.math.ca/Competitions/COMC/2019/results.PREVIEW.html/QC

The Montreal Math Circle is an enrichment program that offers an occasion for students interested in mathematics to solve challenging problems at their grade level. The circle aims at bringing pleasure and confidence in children while doing mathematics. The problems during each meeting will be centered around a specific topic. The instructor is bilingual and will accommodate students from both English and French instruction schools. For more information, please feel free to send an email at montrealmathclub@gmail.com.

Parents of children attending the circle are asked to complete the following form and bring it to the instructor at the beginning of the session:  http://www.concordia.ca/content/dam/common/docs/policies/official-policies/VPS-8_waiver.pdf

Please note: We reserve the right to take photographs during our outreach activities or events and use them for promotional purposes. Thank you for your cooperation.

 Montreal Math Circle is an official writing centre for the Canadian Open Mathematics Challenge (COMC).
