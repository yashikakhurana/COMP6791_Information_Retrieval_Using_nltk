Computer labs - Department of Mathematics & Statistics

Concordia offers a wide range of computing resources, electronic, and media-related equipment to the academic community. The two labs on this page are for use by members of the Department of Mathematics and Statistics only. Eligibility information, hardware/software, and support information are listed within the documentation section of this page.

Who can use it?

These labs are reserved exclusively for use by students enrolled in the Department of Mathematics & Statistics. 

How much does it cost?

There is no cost associated with this service.

How to get it

Access to the labs is restricted based on program and level of study. All students using these labs must be enrolled in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics. For more information about eligibility, visit the documentation section of this page.

Documentation

Undergraduate lab (LB 915-04)

This lab is exclusively available to undergraduate students enrolled in the Department of Mathematics & Statistics.

Hardware:
30 desktop computers
1 printer

Software:
Maple, Mathematica, MikTeX, Minitab, PCTeX, R for Windows, Scientific Notebook, Statistical Analysis Software (SAS), and Statistical Package for Social Science (SPSS)

Support:
For technical support in the undergraduate lab, contact the Service Desk:
Email: help@concordia.ca
Phone: 514-848-2424, ext. 7613

For urgent lab support, contact the Service Centre:
Walk-in: H-421
Phone: 514-848-2424, ext. 3435

Graduate lab (LB 915-02)

This lab is exclusively available to graduate students enrolled in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics.

Hardware:
18 desktop computers
1 printer

Software:
Maple, MikTex, Minitab, R for Windows, Statistical Analysis Software (SAS), and Statistical Package for Social Science (SPSS).

Support:
For technical support in the undergraduate lab, contact the Service Desk:
Email: help@concordia.ca
Phone: 514-848-2424, ext. 7613

For urgent lab support, contact the Service Centre:
Walk-in: H-421
Phone: 514-848-2424, ext. 3435
