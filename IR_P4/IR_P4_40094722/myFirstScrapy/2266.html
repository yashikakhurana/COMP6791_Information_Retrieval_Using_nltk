Are you looking to get involved in other initiatives at Concordia?

  • To find volunteering, work, internship, athletic and other opportunities, visit Concordia's Beyond the Classroom section.

Many services are available to support you throughout your studies at Concordia University.

  • Birks Student Service Centre: student ID, tuition payment and refunds, institutional forms
  • Career and Planning Services: information, workshops, and events about career preparation
  • Faculty of Arts and Science's Student Academic Services: advising on your program, registration, exchange opportunities, academic difficulties, and degree audit
  • Health & wellness services: counselling and development, health services (including medical clinic and testing), and wellness support
  • International Student Office: comprehensive support for international students, including visa and permits, health insurance information, and workshops.
  • Student Success Centre: learning support, new student services, student advocacy and mentoring, FutureReady skill development program, and career counselling.
