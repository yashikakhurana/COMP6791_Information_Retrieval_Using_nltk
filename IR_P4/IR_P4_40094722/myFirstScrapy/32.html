Discover our workshops

 

Get up to 50% reimbursement of expenses related to professional development trainings with CCE’s workshops. Read more about this opportunity.

Enroll in CCE’s online workshops to improve your skills, develop your technical knowledge, benefit from unique opportunities and grow both professionally and personally. We've got you covered with just about any topic you may be looking for.

All online workshop offerings may be customized and adapted to your teams’ specific needs and business reality, in English or in French, on the day and time of your choosing, and remotely, in real-time.
