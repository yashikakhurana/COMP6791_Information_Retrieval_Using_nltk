Security & privacy

Cybersecurity is everyone’s responsibility. Taking basic precautions helps to safeguard personal data, research findings and financial information.

Failing to do so puts an individual at risk as well as the colleagues and institutions to which they are connected.

Warning about COVID-19-related cybersecurity threats

Training for staff and faculty

To help protect the Concordia community from data breaches, hacks and ransomware attacks, IITS is providing a new training module for all staff and faculty.

 

Please access the MediaPro training through MyConcordia or by clicking on this secure link. Sign on with your Concordia netname and password. All Concordia faculty and staff should complete the 20-minute course.

NEW! Multi-factor authentication for faculty and staff

Concordia is adopting multi-factor authentication (MFA) to keep you safe and reduce the risk of having your account accessed by someone other than yourself.
