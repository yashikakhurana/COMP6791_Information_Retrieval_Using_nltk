University 101: How Does This All Work?

Share this event:
Date & time
Monday, January 18, 2021
10 a.m. – 11:30 a.m.
Other dates

Tuesday, December 15, 2020
Friday, January 15, 2021

Cost

This event is free

Website

Student Success Centre

Contact

Services for New Students
514-848-2424 ext. 7369

Where

Online

Submit an event
Latest news

University is a totally different reality from what you may have experienced in high school or CEGEP. It’s a whole different system, with a different set of rules and expectations, and it comes with a whole new set of perks as well as challenges that can sometimes leave you feeling a bit lost. This workshop will help get you familiarized with university culture, clarify some common university terminology you should know about, and outline how some of the essential aspects of university life work so you can take on your first year with confidence.

Open to all new undergraduate, independent or transfer students.

Registration is required.
The session takes place via Zoom.  

Facilitator name:

Monica Boulos, c.o., M.A.
