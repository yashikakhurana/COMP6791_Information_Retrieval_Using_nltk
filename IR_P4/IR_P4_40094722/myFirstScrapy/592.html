OFFICE OF THE DEAN

The Office of the Dean team sets academic priorities for the Faculty of Arts and Science (FAS) and manages the faculty in accordance with collective agreements and other university policies.

Pascale Sicotte
Dean, Faculty of Arts and Science
dean.artsci@concordia.ca

View profile
Contact the Dean's staff

Jill Didur 
Associate Dean, Faculty Affairs

The Associate Dean, Faculty Affairs oversees the academic personnel portfolio, including matters related to the CUFA (full-time faculty) and CUPFA (part-time faculty) collective agreements.

adfa.fas@concordia.ca

View faculty profile

John Capobianco 
Associate Dean, Planning and Academic Facilities 

The Associate Dean, Planning and Academic Facilities, is responsible for coordinating all aspects of facilities planning and space management in conjunction with the academic departments and units as well as with Facilities Management and the office of the Vice-Provost.

adpaf.fas@concordia.ca

View faculty profile

Philippe Caignon 
Associate Dean, Student Academic Services 

The Associate Dean, Student Academic Services (SAS) leads advising, enrolment, retention and all other administrative tasks concerning the undergraduate students in the faculty, including post-admission transfer credits and advising.

adsas.fas@concordia.ca

View faculty profile

Richard Courtemanche 
Associate Dean, Academic Programs

The Associate Dean, Academic Programs oversees all matters related to the development and revision, whether major or minor, undergraduate or graduate, of academic programs for the Faculty of Arts and Science.

adapro.fas@concordia.ca

View faculty profile

Patrick Leroux 
Associate Dean, Research

The Associate Dean, Research is responsible for promoting, enhancing and facilitating research through strategic leadership that supports the faculty’s research goals.

adr.fas@concordia.ca

View faculty profile

Francesca Scala 
Associate Dean, Graduate Studies

The Associate Dean, Graduate Studies oversees matters related to recruitment, admissions and retention; develops policies and best practices to enrich graduate student experience; develops guidelines for fellowship and award allocation and oversees the adjudication of faculty-wide scholarships and awards in collaboration with the School of Graduate Studies and academic units. 

adgs.fas@concordia.ca

View faculty profile
