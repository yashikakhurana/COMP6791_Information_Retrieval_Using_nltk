Lockers

2021 WINTER SESSION RENTALS

Due to current circumstances, beginning January,
you can rent a locker for a one-term winter session
Locker rental and fee.
January 11 - April 30, 2021
 

Locker rentals made easy!

LOCKER TERM: SEPTEMBER 1 - JULY 31
Select Rent a Locker below.

Select Lockers to Rent, Renew, View or Modify your existing locker data.

Lockers are available for rent in buildings across both campuses.
Its real simple to rent a locker online - and you can select the location by campus, building, and floor.

Rent a Locker
You can rent a new locker as of September 1 for the Sept 1 – July 31 term

Renew a Locker
You can renew your existing locker rental for the next term at any time prior to the July 31 end date
Renewing your locker allows you to retain your locker in August (for free) throughout the next rental term

Locker Content
Locker contents which remain in the lockers past the rental expiration date (July 31) are immediately discarded (unless the locker was renewed).

Combination Locks
Combination locks are provided by the University for all lockers. You cannot use/place a personal lock on the lockers.

Locker Cancellation
No refunds are given for cancelled/returned lockers.

Rental Payment method
Credit Card Payment(s) only.

Rent a Locker Now

Using a combination lock or if you forget your combination number

If you've never used a combination lock, see easy instruction below on how to open one:

First, You will receive a combination number which opens the lock. Let's say your combination number is: 25-11-33

Firmly push the handles all the way into the base and follow the steps:

  1. Turn three whole turns to the right (–>) and stop at the first number (25)
  2. Turn one whole turn to the left (<–), passing your first number, and stop at the second number (11)
  3. Turn right (–>) and stop at the last number (33)
  4. You're done. Follow the same for the combination numbers you are given and your lock should open!

You can also check out the video on how to open a combination lock.

If you've followed the instructions carefully, and your lock still won't open

Email lockers@concordia.ca and we'll send someone over to verify and/or change the lock.
If we change the lock, we will put the new combination number on the Portal, in your locker data.

If you forget your combination number, please note that combination numbers will NOT be given over the phone or email.

1. You can log on to your locker Portal and select: View my locker data.

or, if you cannot access your data...

2. You (and only if you are the person who registered for the locker) must present yourself at the Locker service office, in person with ID, during regular business hours. You cannot send someone else on your behalf.

 

 

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

RENT A LOCKER - using DESKTOP

Click on +New Rental

Step 1 – Find Locker

Select locker criteria (from building, floor, locker type) and click Search

Available lockers will appear in the grid below
Select the preferred locker and click the Next button 

Step 2 – Rental Term and Payment

Rental term details and cost appears  

Scroll to read and agree to the Terms and Conditions

Your must click in the I agree box to continue
Enter Credit card details

Click on PayNow

See confirmation that locker was rented (and view locker number and combination).

Rent additional Lockers

Repeat the same steps as above.

RENT A LOCKER – using IPHONE, ANDROID, IPAD or TAB

Click on +New Rental

Step 1 – Find Locker

Select locker criteria (from building, floor, locker type) and click Search

Step 2 –  Select Locker

Available lockers will appear in the grid below
Select the preferred locker and click the Rent this Locker

Step 3 - Rental Term and Payment

Select Rental term

Details and cost appears  

Your must agree to the Terms and Conditions by clicking the I agree box to continue
Enter Credit card details

Click on PayNow

See Thank you/Confirmation page that locker was rented

Rent additional Lockers

Repeat the same steps as above.

VIEW RENTED LOCKERS AND COMBINATION NUMBER

At anytime on the application Student Home page, you will be able to view all lockers rented in your name.

See locker number and rental end dates.

Click on Combination number and it will appear.

RENEW A LOCKER

On the application Student Home page, in the box where your locker appears, click on Renew
Select the Rental term
Complete the payment process
See confirmation that your locker was renewed (view rental end date).

CANCEL A LOCKER

On the application Student Home page, in the box where your locker appears, click on Return
Enter RETURN  in the box and follow the steps 
Indicate the reason you are returning the locker in the Return Reason text box

Your locker will be removed from the list.
There are no refunds.
You must remove all locker contents immediately once you have returned your locker, otherwise all items will be discarded and lost.

CANNOT OPEN LOCK

Please see “How to open a combination Lock” - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kmx4WCAj77A

If you’ve tried this and the lock still does not open please send and email to lockers@concordia.ca

Subject: Defective lock.  Indicate your name, student ID, locker number, and lock serial number

Someone will go on site to verify the lock. We will advise you once the new lock combination number appears in the locker rental application.

OPEN A COMBINATION LOCK

Example combination number 25-11-33: follow these steps:
1. Firmly push the handles all the way into the base
2. Turn three whole turns to the right (–>) and stop at the first number (25)
3. Turn one whole turn to the left (<–) all the way around once, and then come to the second number (11)
4. Turn right (–>) and stop at the last number (33)
Follow these steps with your number and your locker should open.

CANNOT OPEN LOCK ANYMORE (But it used to OPEN)

Your lock may have become defective.
Send an email to lockers@concordia.ca 
Subject: Defective lock.  Indicate your name, student ID, locker number.

Someone will go on site to verify the lock. We will advise you once the new lock combination number appears in the locker rental application.

CHANGE LOCKER WITHOUT ADDITIONAL COST (first two weeks of Rental)

You will not be charged an additional rental IF you change your locker within the first two weeks of the rental purchase date.
On the application Student Home page, in the box where your locker appears, click on Return
Enter RETURN (all Caps) in the box and follow the steps 
Send an email to lockers@concordia.ca:
1. Indicate your name, Student ID number, locker number that you cancelled
2.  indicate the number and/or location for the new locker you wish to rent
We will rent the locker for you (no charge) so you will have only paid for one locker
We will advise you once the new locker information appears on the locker application.
Important: If contents are not removed from the old locker within five days, all items will be discarded.

THE LOCK HAS BEEN CHANGED (Cannot access locker)

Once a rental date expires, all locker contents are immediately removed and discarded, and the lock is changed, as per the Locker Rental Terms and Agreement.
If your rental date had not expired and you cannot get into the locker:
Send an email to lockers@concordia.ca 
Indicate your name, student ID, locker number.

We will verify your dossier, as well as the lock which may have become defective, and inform you accordingly.

LOCKER DOOR IS SEALED AND WILL NOT OPEN

The locker may have a double lock feature. 
Try to open the locker lift and pull the metal plate located just above the lock.
If this does not open your locker, send an email to: lockers@concordia.ca
Indicate your ID number, and the locker number
We will rent a new locker for you (no additional rental fee).
We will advise you once the new locker information appears in the locker application. 
You will have five days to Cancel your locker. To cancel, select the locker and click on Return to proceed.
Important: If contents are not removed from the old locker within five days, all items will be discarded.

 

Faculty and staff wishing to rent a locker for their business activities are welcome to contact us at lockers@concordia.ca to make all necessary arrangements.
